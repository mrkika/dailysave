Terms & Conditions

Welcome to Daily Save! By registering or using the Daily Save application (“App”), you agree to be bound by these Terms & Conditions (“T&C”). Please read them carefully before proceeding.

1. Definitions

  • “User” means any individual who registers for or uses the App.
  • “Plan” means the daily savings plan a User selects at the beginning of each month.
  • “Payment Proof” means an uploaded receipt (e.g., POS screenshot) confirming that a User has made the daily contribution.
  • “Collector” refers to the individual or administrator who verifies Payment Proofs and approves them in the system.
  • “Withdrawal” means the User’s request to receive their net savings back, after deducting the collector’s fee.

2. User Registration & Account

  1. Users must register with a valid phone number, email address, and password.
  2. Upon registration, each User is assigned a unique Account ID. This ID must be used when making deposits to ensure payments are properly attributed.
  3. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials. Any activity under a User’s account is their sole responsibility.

3. Monthly Contribution Plans

  1. At the start of each calendar month, a User must select a daily contribution amount (for example, ₦500, ₦1,000, ₦5,000, etc.).
  2. Once selected, the chosen daily amount remains fixed for the entire month. Users cannot change their plan mid‐month. To change, they must wait until the next calendar month begins.
  3. Each Plan corresponds to the number of days in that specific month (e.g., 28–31 days). Users are encouraged to contribute every single day of the month.

4. Payment Proof & Approval

  1. Every day, the User makes their cash or POS deposit into the collector’s account, using their Account ID as the payment reference.
  2. After payment, the User must upload a clear image of their payment receipt (Payment Proof) to the App. Each Payment Proof is marked “Pending” until verified by the Collector.
  3. The Collector reviews Payment Proofs and marks each as Approved or Rejected:
    • Approved: Payment is valid, and that day is counted as “Paid”.
    • Rejected: Payment is invalid or receipt is unclear. The User will receive a “Rejected” notification and can re‐upload a correct proof.
  4. Users may pay ahead for future dates. Once a future date’s proof is approved, that date is marked “Paid” even before it arrives.

5. Collector’s Fee & Payout

  1. Regardless of how many days a User actually pays (whether they skip days or pay in advance), the Collector’s fee for that User each month equals exactly one day’s contribution amount. For example, if a User’s daily amount is ₦1,000, the Collector’s fee for that month is a flat ₦1,000.
  2. Total Paid = (Number of Approved Payment Proofs) × (Daily Amount).
    Collector Fee = (Daily Amount).
    Refund = Total Paid − Collector Fee.
  3. Users may only request a Withdrawal on the 25th day of each month. This policy encourages Users to contribute for as many days as possible before receiving any payout. Withdrawals requested on any other date will not be processed.
  4. Any Withdrawal request submitted on the 25th day is subject to Collector review. Once approved, the net amount will be disbursed via bank transfer or mobile money within 48 hours.

6. Deposit Requests

  1. If a User chooses to make a lump‐sum deposit (for multiple days or the entire month), they can generate a Deposit Reference from the App. That reference is unique and must be given to the Collector.
  2. The Collector will monitor pending Deposit Requests and verify payment once the User uploads proof. Users must upload Payment Proof immediately after depositing.
  3. Failure to upload Payment Proof within 24 hours of deposit may result in the Deposit Request being canceled or rejected.

7. User Obligations

  1. Users agree to upload clear, unaltered Payment Proofs. Any attempt to forge or tamper with receipts is strictly prohibited.
  2. Users are responsible for ensuring their Payment Proof images are legible. Rejected proofs may delay the counting of that day as “Paid.”
  3. Users must maintain accurate contact information (phone and email) so that notifications and reminders can reach them.

8. Privacy & Data Security

  1. Daily Save collects only the information necessary to provide its services: phone number, email, first/last name, and uploaded Payment Proofs.
  2. All user‐uploaded images (Payment Proof) are stored securely. We do not share, sell, or otherwise disclose user data to third parties without explicit consent, except when required by law.
  3. Users retain the right to request deletion of their account and data by contacting support at support@dailysave.com. Upon deletion, all associated Payment Proofs, Deposit Requests, Withdrawal Requests, and Profile data will be permanently removed.

9. Limitations of Liability

  1. Daily Save acts only as a digital ledger and does not handle money directly. All cash or POS payments occur outside the App, into a shared Collector account that mirrors existing community practices.
  2. Although we strive to ensure accurate tracking, we are not responsible for human errors—such as a Collector failing to approve a valid proof in a timely manner. Users should keep backup receipts in case of disputes.
  3. Daily Save is not liable for any loss or damage arising from User negligence, fraud, or misuse of the App.
