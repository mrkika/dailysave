Privacy Policy

At Daily Save (“we,” “us,” or “our”), protecting your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application (“App”). By accessing or using Daily Save, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  1. Account Information: When you register, we collect your full name, phone number, email address, and a password. We store this to create and manage your account.
  2. Profile Data: You may upload a profile photo and specify whether you are a “Client” or “Collector.”
  3. Payment Proofs: When you upload POS or cash receipt images, we store those files (as PaymentProof records) to verify your daily contributions.
  4. Plan Information: Each month you choose a daily contribution amount (e.g., ₦1,000). We store your selected amount and the month/year as a ContributionPlan.
  5. Deposit & Withdrawal Requests: If you generate a deposit reference or request a withdrawal, we store those records (DepositRequest / WithdrawRequest) along with timestamps.
  6. Usage Data: We automatically collect certain information when you use the App, including IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and timestamps. This helps us monitor performance and detect abuse.

2. How We Use Your Information

  1. Account Management: To create, authenticate, and manage your user account so you can access the App’s features.
  2. Payment Verification: To review and approve/reject daily payment proofs, ensure accurate tracking of contributions, and compute your refund at month’s end.
  3. Transaction Records: To record Deposit Requests and Withdrawal Requests, generate unique references, and notify Collectors when action is required.
  4. Communication: To send you in‐App notifications, email confirmations, password resets, and occasional reminders (e.g., to upload today’s proof). You can opt out of non‐essential communications.
  5. Security & Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent unauthorized access, fraudulent activity, or abuse by monitoring login patterns and verifying uploaded files.
  6. Analytics & Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, improve our services, and fix bugs. We may use aggregated, anonymized data for internal reporting and performance optimization.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

  1. Internal Teams & Collectors: Your payment proofs and requested Deposit/Withdrawal References are visible to Collectors (staff) for verification and approval. This is necessary to operate the App’s core functionality.
  2. Third‐Party Service Providers: We may share your data (e.g., email address, payment confirmation) with trusted providers who help us run the App—such as hosting, email delivery, or file storage services. All third parties are contractually bound to keep your data confidential.
  3. Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests (court orders, subpoenas) or to protect our rights, users, or the public.
  4. With Your Consent: If you explicitly request us to share your data with a third party (for example, your bank or accountant), we will do so only after obtaining your consent.
  5. Aggregated/Anonymized Data: We may publish aggregate statistics (e.g., total savings collected) that do not identify any specific user.

4. Data Security & Retention

  1. Storage: Payment Proof images and profile photos are stored on secure servers. Access is restricted only to authorized personnel (Collectors and Admins).
  2. Encryption: All data transmissions between your device and our servers are encrypted via HTTPS (TLS).
  3. Retention: We retain your account information, payment proofs, and transaction records for as long as your account remains active. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data and associated payment proofs within 30 days, except where legally required to retain certain records (e.g., tax compliance).
  4. Data Breaches: In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify you and relevant authorities within 72 hours, as required by applicable law, and take steps to mitigate potential harm.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

  1. Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (e.g., localStorage) to maintain your session, remember preferences, and enhance user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser, but certain features (like staying logged in) may not work properly.
  2. Analytics: We use basic analytics (e.g., Google Analytics or a self‐hosted solution) to understand how users interact with the App. All analytics data is aggregated and anonymized—no personally identifiable information is shared.

6. Child Privacy

Daily Save is not intended for use by anyone under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will promptly delete that account and associated data.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  1. Access & Correction: You can view and update your profile information (name, email, profile photo) anytime via the “Profile” page.
  2. Withdrawal of Consent: You can opt out of non‐essential communications (e.g., reminders) via email settings or in‐App preferences. Core transactional emails (payment confirmations, deposit references) cannot be opted out of, as they are necessary for functionality.
  3. Account Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting support@dailysave.com. We will erase your personal data within 30 days, except any records we must retain for legal compliance (e.g., payment history for audit).

8. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services (for example, payment processors). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  1. We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. When we do, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page (below the header).
  2. We will notify you in‐App of any material changes. Continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
